Announcement:

Tightline Computers is dead!

Long Live Tightline Computers!

     

Tightline Computers was incorporated in the state of Ohio, 
USA, in October 2001with Marcia G Akins as President and 
Andy Kramek as Vice President. The new company takes over 
from Tightline Computers Ltd which has operated primarily in 
the UK and Europe since 1993. It is committed to providing 
continuing support and assistance for all clients of 
Tightline Computers Ltd.
