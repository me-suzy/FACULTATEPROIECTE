1) Care sunt muzicienii care au cantat in formatia YES ?
	SELECT NumeMuz
	From Muzicieni M, Formatii F,Discografie D,Realizare Disc R
	Where M.idmuz = R.idmuz and D.idformatie = F.idformatie
and denformatie = 'Yes'

3) Care este formatia care in anii 60 a scos cele mai multe discuri ?
	SELECT Denformatie,SUM(nrBuc) or disc vandute
	From Formatii F INNER JoIN Discografie d on F.idform = D.idformatie
INNER JOIN Vanzari discuri ON D.iddisc = vd.iddisc
Where ON between 1960 AND 1969
Group By DenForm
	Having discvandute >=ALL

12) Sa se afiseze nr de piese continute de fiecare disc al formatiei Queen?
		SELECT titludisc ,COUNT(idpiesa) AS nr.piese
		From...
		Where denformatie = 'Queen'
		GROUP BY titludisc,dataapa
		ORDER BY dataapa

13) Cate discuri a lansat formatia Queen ?
		SELECT COUNT(iddisc) AS nrdiscuri
		From Discografie D INNER JOIN Formatie F ON D.idformatie = F.idformatie
